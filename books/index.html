Nightfaring: In Search of the Disappearing Darkness

A profound travel memoir following a dark sky advocate from New Zealand to Uzbekistan, Italy to the Himalaya -- exploring how humans have depended on, feared, and mythologized darkness throughout history.

Lynne Peeples
